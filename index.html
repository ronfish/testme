About YARD Direct

Going the extra YARD 

YARD Direct is the only UK company that specialises in supplying high quality roof windows and all related products needed to bring more light into your home, office. loft conversion or extension. We are also the cheapest in the UK for any roof skylight product you are looking for and our manufacturers are all trusted names who make the best windows to suit all budgets and tastes - be it VELUX Windows, RoofLITE Skylights, Velux Blinds, Mardome Trade Domes and much more.  We have a price beat promise too to demonstrate our commitment to being the most competitive supplier of pitched roof windows, skylights, suntunnels, flat roof windows and roof domes as well as our own branded product YARDLITE.  Most of these products are available on a two working-day lead time.

Cheapest VELUX, RoofLITE, Mardome, Sunsquare

Crucially, the highly experienced YARD Direct team is available 7 days a week from 8am till 10pm and can give you the best technical advice whether it's about the most suitable type of window and flashing or help with any issues that arise with planning restrictions.

Whether you are looking for solar, electric roof-lights or a flat roof solution for your extension, office or home, the YARD team are here with top quality knowledge and experience to guide you every step of the way. With the full range of VELUX roof windows, including VELUX's Flat Glass Flat Roof Skylight , and INTEGRA Electric Roof Windows, we can get the most of your product needs to you in 48hours. To offer our customers more flexibility and choice, we also stock the VELUX alternatives RoofLITE, Mardome and Sunsquare products.

We also have YARD Connect - our growing UK wide network of VELUX and YARD approved installers.

YARD Direct is a safe and secure site. All payment and delivery details will remain confidential with the aid of PayPal and RBS Worldpay for added security measures.
